My name is Giovanni Navach, and I am currently a student at University of California Riverside (UCR) on the Pre-Business track and looking to specialize in Information Systems. I am born and raised in LA, in the San Fernando valley, and went to high school at Granada Hills Charter. All throughout high school I volunteered and joined clubs to figure out what I wanted to do in life, and fell in love with business and all the things you can branch out into. I have worked one job before, and volunteered as a tutor, in the food pantry, in a warehouse packing food for soldiers, and countless other things over the years. I worked in the food industry as the Front of House at Stonefire Grill, and enjoyed my time there. I learned valuable lessons in the customer service industry, and I am glad I got to experience it.

I also joined volunteer and academic clubs throughout highschool, such as the California Scholarship Federation (CSF), the National Honor Society (NHS), and Key Club and Model United Nations (MUN).
